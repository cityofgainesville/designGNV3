Our Theory of Change

How does this impact the people we serve?






Individual

Design transparent, just-in-time services that are meaningful and impactful to the everyday life of a person living in Gainesville.

Group

If we look at Maslow's hierarch of needs - we need to work on the basics first - the core needs (physiologocal, safety). If our community stops struggling with these, we can work on a higher echelon - belonging.

Society

When we free ourselves of struggling through the transactional business of living, we start to imagine a society where people are empowered, creative m fulfilled and connected.
